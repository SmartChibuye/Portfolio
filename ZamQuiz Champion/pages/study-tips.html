Elevate Your Learning

Expert strategies to help you conquer your exams and master every subject.

Core Strategies

The Pomodoro Technique

Study in focused 25-minute bursts followed by a 5-minute break. This keeps your brain fresh and prevents fatigue.

Active Recall

Instead of just reading, test yourself. Close the book and try to summarize what you just learned out loud.

Mind Mapping

Draw connections between topics. Visualizing how ICT relates to Science helps solidify your understanding.
